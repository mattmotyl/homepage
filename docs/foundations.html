Data Foundations

The Digital Services Act obligates Very Large Online Platforms and Search Engines to provide vetted researchers and civil society partners with meaningful access to systemic risk data. Use this catalog to understand what’s available, where to request it, and how to stay current.

How to stay current

Subscribe to official changelogs

Most platforms maintain RSS feeds or release notes. Add them to your research ops tracker to catch schema updates early.

Join transparency communities

Working groups such as EDMO task forces and Mozilla’s Observatory community often share early intel on dataset releases.

Document your requests

Track when you submit Article 40 requests and the exact datasets you receive. This makes renewal conversations faster.
