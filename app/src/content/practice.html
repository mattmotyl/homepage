Practice

Hands-on time cements the concepts from earlier chapters. This page offers structured exercises, reusable prompts, and a sandbox seeded with the same synthetic data used throughout the primer. Work through the scenarios in order or cherry-pick the questions that align with your research interests.

Suggested exercises

Trace a systemic risk

Identify a delegated harm you care about—such as disinformation targeting a vulnerable community—and map the signals you would need from fact_engagements and the available dimensions. Document which filters isolate the population and which metrics reveal the pattern.

Validate an internal claim

Platforms often cite high-level statistics in public updates. Recreate one of those statements using the sandbox (for example, “shares of long-form videos declined in Q1”). Pay attention to how you aggregate, and note any assumptions you must make along the way.

Design a monitoring alert

Use the probabilistic score tables to define an alerting rule that would surface emerging risks. Consider thresholds, sample sizes, and how frequently you would rerun the query.
