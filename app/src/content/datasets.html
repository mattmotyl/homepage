7.2 Tips for responsible reuse

Document provenance

Note who collected the data, under what terms, and whether you must attribute or notify them when publishing findings. Many civic-tech projects rely on volunteer labor—acknowledging their work sustains the ecosystem.

Respect licensing

Some repositories allow unrestricted reuse; others require you to keep derivatives non-commercial or to delete data after a set window. When in doubt, email the maintainers before redistributing.

Cross-check with Article 40 access

Use public datasets to refine your hypotheses and to test code. When you receive official platform data, compare metrics to ensure your transformations are working and to surface gaps in public transparency.
